Double bed jacquard (DBJ) as described here (Rib Jacquard with birds-eye backing) is known in Brother manuals as Multi-Colour Rib.
See pages 38-39 of the KR850 ribber manual (the penguins) and page 11 of the manual for the KRC-900 color changer.
Carriage part names are defined on page 32 of the KH910 manual and on page 1 of the KR850 manual, respectively.
There are a number of different variants of DBJ but the cast on method is the same.

Smiley was made from this image:

AYAB takes care of the colors for you, you just give it a black-and-white (or grayscale) pattern as if you are going to do single bed Fair Isle.

To create your own patterns, refer to section "Pattern creation". Use thin yarns, you'll be using side-by-side needles on both beds. (For those in the US, Woollike yarn works well.)
